# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

MF_Website serves visitors who are forming a first impression of the Miaofeng platform: consumers looking for seedlings and planting knowledge, growers who may need professional guidance, and merchants considering entry into the platform. They arrive in a brand-discovery context, not a transaction-heavy workflow.

## Product Purpose

MF_Website is the ecological brand website for the Miaofeng platform. It uses immersive nature storytelling to build a green, professional, trustworthy first impression, then guides visitors toward the main platform entries: nursery mall, tree encyclopedia, MF Forest Pet, AI consultation, and merchant onboarding.

## Brand Personality

Natural, professional, companionable. The interface should feel like a calm forest-system brand with practical agricultural credibility, not a generic environmental campaign page or a hard-selling ecommerce landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid a plain white corporate website, a generic public-welfare environmental page disconnected from seedlings or fertilizer commerce, a heavy navigation bar that breaks the first-screen immersion, slogan-heavy marketing blocks, and a complex pet game system that distracts from the website's brand and platform-entry role.

## Design Principles

- Keep the forest narrative as the primary first impression.
- Make Miaofeng identifiable within the first viewport.
- Treat MF Forest Pet as a branded companion and AI entry, not as a normal nav link.
- Keep business entrances visible but lightweight.
- Preserve the existing GSAP/Lenis immersive scroll experience unless a focused change requires otherwise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Navigation and floating pet controls should be keyboard reachable, have readable labels, and maintain adequate contrast on image backgrounds. Mobile controls should preserve comfortable tap targets, safe-area spacing, and reduced visual obstruction. Motion should remain purposeful and should not make core navigation unusable.
